WHO ARE WE ?

Africa Productivity Specialists

Africa Productivity Specialists is an operations and productivity improvement institution established to implement the productivity agenda on the African continent. Our objective is to promote the mainstreaming of continuous productivity improvement across all economic sectors. Ultimately, the continent must understand that its greatness is embedded in its collective effort and that AFRICA IS ONE SYSTEM.
