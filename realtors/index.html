About T H C

Founded in 2019 in Namibia, T H C has grown from a small Namibian startup to one of Namibia’s leading digital travel companies, T H C’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience Namibia.

By investing in the technology that helps take the friction out of travel, T H C seamlessly connects millions of travellers with memorable experiences, a range of incredible places to stay - from homes to hotels and much more. As one of Namibia’s largest travel marketplaces for both established brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, T H C enables properties all over Namibia to reach a National audience and grow their businesses.
