About the Academy

“Mino” is derived from an all-female military regiment of the 17th century Kingdom of the Dahomey Amazon. A rich history of women lead defence across the continent. The Mino Cyber Academy, programme is putting women on the frontline of cyber security in Africa.

Cyber security is a major threat to natural and legal entities globally.

And within the academy one can:

  • Develop scarce skills through cyber security training with the Capacity to train 80 cyber security professionals per year
  • Deal with security issues at an organizational, national and continental level.
  • Acquire the tools and solutions for cyber defense Empowering African organizations to manage cyber- attacks with best practice solutions
  • Training opportunity exclusively for Africa’s young women.
