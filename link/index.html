About Afro Dot

The AU Convention on Cybersecurity and Personal Data Protection sets

“the essential rules for establishing a credible digital environment (cyber space) and address the gaps affecting the regulation and legal recognition of electronic communications and electronic signature; as well as the absence of specific legal rules that protects consumers, intellectual property rights, personal data and information systems and privacy online.” Namibia is amongst AU member countries that have ratified this Convention. As a Namibian cybersecurity company, it is therefore our responsibility to ensure that the country is contributing to this continental goal of having a secure and credible digital environment.
