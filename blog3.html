The Delight

Located in Swakopmund, 1.5 km from Palm Beach, The Delight Swakopmund features a restaurant and free WiFi. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free private parking is available on site. Each room at this hotel is air-conditioned and has a flat-screen TV. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and The Delight Swakopmund offers packed lunches. Mermaid Casino and Atlanta cinema are 100 m from The Delight Swakopmund, while Desert Tracks is 200 m from the property. The nearest airport is Walvis Bay Airport, 48 km from the property.

Lodge | Available
West Nest Lodge

The lodge offers a variety of accommodation ranging from chalets to tents. The rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower while the tents have shared bathroom facilities. At West Nest Lodge guests can enjoy outdoor activities such as putt-putt golf and a small rock climbing wall. There is also a restaurant as well as a bar. The lodge is situated on Farm Klein Begin, 11 km from the B6 Trans Kalahari Highway and 35 km west of Gobabis. Hosea Kutako Airport is 125 km away.

Hotel Uhland

Decorated in warm and bright colours, rooms at Hotel Pension Uhland are equipped with a TV set, en-suite bathrooms and a work desk. Self-catering facilities include a kettle and a minibar for beverages. A breakfast buffet consisting of fresh fruits, typical Namibian smoked meat and homemade jams is included and served daily. In the afternoons guests can sample tartlets to go with tea, while the pool bar offers local drinks. The hotel has a library where it is possible to exchange books and provides Namibian newspapers in English and German. It is also possible to get a local SIM card on arrival, or book an airport shuttle service. Situated only 500 m from Windhoekâ€™s Railway station, Pension Uhland offers on-site private parking.

Vista Mare

Vista Mare, a property with a terrace, is set in Swakopmund, 701 m from The Mole Beach, 0.7 mi from Artist Arcade Elton Mugomo, as well as 0.9 mi from Otavi-Bahnhof. This beachfront property offers access to a balcony, free private parking and free WiFi. The apartment is fitted with 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a patio with sea views. If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible in the surroundings and the apartment can arrange a car rental service. Popular points of interest near Vista Mare include Atlanta cinema, National Marine Aquarium and Hohenzollernhaus. The nearest airport is Walvis Bay Airport, 22 mi from the accommodations. This is our guests' favorite part of Swakopmund, according to independent reviews.
